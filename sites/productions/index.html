What we do

West Peek Productions is a specialized virtual and hybrid event production company founded in 2020 by Scooter Taylor. We partner with organizations to design and execute online experiences that feel intentional, professional, and human.

Our team has produced more than 400 virtual and hybrid events — including conferences, summits, galas, pitch competitions, board meetings, career fairs, and community gatherings — for clients ranging from startups to Fortune 500 organizations.

How it works

Before the event

Platform selection, technical planning, rehearsals, and speaker preparation. We design the system so nothing feels improvised on the day of the event.

During the event

Live production, visuals, graphics, audio, camera direction, and real‑time troubleshooting. Our role is to keep things calm and invisible.

After the event

Recordings, post‑event assets, follow‑ups, and debriefs so one event becomes long‑term leverage.

Who we work with

Marketing and communications leaders, event professionals, foundations, nonprofits, educational institutions, and cross‑functional teams inside larger organizations.
